# Methodology: Shadow Friction Index ($SFI$)

The SFI is a quantitative measure of subconscious resistance to scaling. It is calculated through 12 specific data points (questions), mapping to 4 core scenarios.

## 1. VITALITY (Энергия)
**Node ID: 01**
- **Definition**: The raw thermodynamic resource of the system.
- **Key Indicators**: Sleep quality vs. output, caffeine/stimulant dependency, "Sunday night" anxiety levels.
- **Friction Type**: Resource depletion.

## 2. SOVEREIGN (Суверенность)
**Node ID: 02**
- **Definition**: The internal decision-making authority.
- **Key Indicators**: Sensitivity to "expert" opinions, boundary integrity with partners/employees, guilt-based decision making.
- **Friction Type**: Authority leakage.

## 3. EXPANSION (Экспансия)
**Node ID: 03**
- **Definition**: The system's capacity to absorb growth without triggering "Safe Mode".
- **Key Indicators**: "Accidental" technical glitches during launches, sudden health issues when income spikes, procrastination on high-leverage tasks.
- **Friction Type**: Growth sabotage.

## 4. ARCHITECT (Архитектор)
**Node ID: 04**
- **Definition**: The structural integrity and systemic logic.
- **Key Indicators**: Reliance on "Brute Force" vs. "System Design", clarity of the 3-year vision, ability to delegate high-IQ tasks.
- **Friction Type**: Structural collapse.

## Scoring Logic
The index ($SFI$) is calculated as the weighted average of friction across these 4 nodes. 
- **0-30%**: Optimal System (Sovereign Flow).
- **31-60%**: Functional Attrition (Shadow Tax applied).
- **61-100%**: System Critical (Sabotage Protocols active).
